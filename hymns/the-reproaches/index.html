The reproaches

Veneration of the Cross

My people, what have I done to you?
How have I offended you?
Answer me!
Holy God, holy and strong!
Holy immortal one, have mercy.

  1. I led you out of Egypt, from slavery to freedom
    But you led your Saviour to the Cross.

    Holy God, holy and strong!
    Holy immortal one, have mercy.

  2. For forty years I led you safely through the desert.
    I fed you with manna from heaven,
    And brought you to a land of plenty;
    But you led your Saviour to the cross.

    Holy God, holy and strong!
    Holy immortal one, have mercy.

  3. What more could I have done for you?
    I planted you as my fairest vine,
    but you yielded only bitterness:
    When I was thirsty you gave me vinegar to drink,
    And with a lance you pierced your Saviour’s side.

    Holy God, holy and strong!
    Holy immortal one, have mercy.

  4. For your sake I scourged your captors and their firstborn sons,
    But you brought your scourges down on me.

    My people, what have I done to you?
    How have I offended you?
    Answer me!

  5. I led you from slavery to freedom
    And drowned your captors in the sea,
    But to your high priests you handed me over.

    My people, what have I done to you?
    How have I offended you?
    Answer me!

  6. I opened the sea before you,
    But you opened my side with a spear.

    My people, what have I done to you?
    How have I offended you?
    Answer me!

  7. I led you on your way in a pillar of cloud,
    But you led me to Pilate’s court.

    My people, what have I done to you?
    How have I offended you?
    Answer me!

  8. With manna in the desert I bore you up,
    But you struck me down and scourged me.

    My people, what have I done to you?
    How have I offended you?
    Answer me!

  9. I gave you saving water from the rock,
    But you gave me gall and vinegar to drink.

    My people, what have I done to you?
    How have I offended you?
    Answer me!

  10. For you I stuck down the kings of Cannan,
    But with a reed you struck my head.

    My people, what have I done to you?
    How have I offended you?
    Answer me!

  11. I gave you a royoal sceptre,
    But you gave me a crown of thorn.

    My people, what have I done to you?
    How have I offended you?
    Answer me!

  12. I raised you to the height of majesty,
    But high on a cross, you raised me.

    My people, what have I done to you?
    How have I offended you?
    Answer me!
